About SFM

SFM Latvia is an Estonian–Latvian joint venture founded in 2010 by 2 experienced manufacturers.

Although still a young company, we have nearly 10 years of experience—and very ambitious plans.

We employ 50 workers. Our welders are EN287 certified. The company is ISO 9001:2008 certified by BM TRADA as well as EN 1090-2, ISO 14001:2004, and OHSAS 18001:2007.

In 2015 our turnover was 3.1 million EUR.
